Anti-infective & Antibiotics

Allergy Management

Lasting relief from allergic rhinitis & urticaria

The LEVOVIBE range combines levocetirizine with montelukast to deliver dual antihistamine and leukotriene receptor antagonist action — providing targeted, long-lasting relief for rhinitis, urticaria, and asthma.

LEVOVIBE LEVOVIBE-M

Female Healthcare

Targeted micronutrient support for women’s health & anaemia

Iron deficiency anaemia remains one of India’s most prevalent nutritional challenges, disproportionately affecting women and adolescent girls. ROZYRED combines Ferric Pyrophosphate with Lactoferrin, Vitamin C, and B12 for superior absorption and comprehensive haematological support.

ROZYRED
